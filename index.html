Overview

Someone who'd rather let the data tell the story

I'm Sandhya Mourya, a final-year BSc IT student graduating in 2026 with a focus on data analytics. My interest in this field started with a simple question — "why did this number move?" — and turned into a habit of digging through spreadsheets, writing SQL queries at odd hours, and building dashboards until the story finally made sense.

I'm currently looking for Data Analyst / Business Analyst roles where I can keep doing exactly this — asking better questions of the data and building the visuals that answer them.
